# Gameplay Period #3

This is a period of gameplay with its own ruleset


Once the Storm has arrived on Earth, players collectively fight the Combine Invasion Force

# One Stage Battle


The Combine Invasion Force is displayed as a large red Health Bar on the site

Incoming Combine attacks are displayed as a list of arrival timers in a separate window

Outgoing Earth attacks are displayed in the same way

# Win Condition

## Combine Victory

- [[$GLOBALDEFENSIVE]] fund falls below threshold

## Earth Victory

- Combine Health Points hit zero


# Attack Targets

Incoming Attacks on Earth:

- The Combine Invasion Force only attacks the [[$GLOBALDEFENSIVE]] fund

Outgoing Attacks from Earth:

- The [[$GLOBALDEFENSIVE]] fund only attacks the Combine Invasion Force

Health Points
