# [[$HLX]] GLOBAL HOPE BUFF


- Hope is a global (de)buff for planet earth in their fight against the combine

- It is applied in combat calculations and impacts damage, health regeneration etc

- It's calculated by measuring the distance to the closest 33.3k 333.3k etc number for $HLX price
	- ranges from a 0.85 debuff to a 3.0 buff on damage calculations
