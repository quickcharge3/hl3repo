This document proposes a cyclical Game Loop that offers players the opportunity to play in two distinct roles ([[Rebellion]] or [[Advisor]]), as well as a *Tribal Wars* *(https://www.tribalwars.co.uk)* inspired [[Combat System]], letting players collectively fight [[Combine Invasion]] and [[Combine Occupation]].

The Game Loop is defined over these separate [[Gameplay Periods]]:

1) [[Peace and Trade]]
2) [[Impending Portal Storm]]
3) [[Combine Invasion]] 
4) [[Combine Occupation]] 
5) [[Rebellion Victory]]
